Summary of Development Activity (Dec 12 - Dec 26, 2024)

This is a summary of the development activity for the past two weeks on the project. Here are the key points:

New features and functionalities:

  • Added support for the Moonlight moth plugin (lhndl93)

This summary highlights the significant progress made on the project in the past two weeks. New features were added, bugs were fixed, and code improvements were implemented.
