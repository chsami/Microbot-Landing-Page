Privacy Policy for Google Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. ("Google"). Google Analytics uses "cookies," which are text files placed on your computer, to help the website analyze how users interact with the site.

Information Collected

Google Analytics collects anonymous information such as the type of device you use, your IP address, the pages you visit, and the duration of your visit. This data helps us improve the website and understand how visitors use it.

How We Use the Data

The data collected through Google Analytics is used to:

  • Monitor and analyze website usage trends
  • Improve the website's performance and user experience
  • Understand user interactions with our content

Data Sharing

Google may share the data collected with third parties when required by law or when third parties process the data on Google's behalf. We do not share any personally identifiable information with Google or any other third parties.

How to Opt-Out

You can opt-out of Google Analytics by disabling cookies in your browser settings or by using the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On.

For more information on how Google uses data collected via Google Analytics, please visit Google's Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Please check this page periodically to stay informed about any changes.
